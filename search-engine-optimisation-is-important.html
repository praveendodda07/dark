Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) is important.


First and foremost. What is SEO and why is it such a big deal. With more than thousand searches per minute, cyberspace is loaded with information. So how do you assure that your company gets seen on it in the top list?

The answer is SEO. Simply put, SEO or search engine optimization is a set of rules for optimizing your website to improve search engine rankings.

No matter what industry your business functions in, having a website has its pros. Consumer behaviour has changed over time. Initially, they would refer to the infamous yellow pages and now that same research goes into web space because they found it to be a hassle-free version of the yellow pages.

“It is a proven fact that users trust searches that appear on the top of their screen far better so having your business featured at the top of their search makes your brand trustworthy and one of their first choice. SEO is important for the smooth running of a big web site. Web sites with more than one author can benefit from SEO in a direct and indirect way. Their direct benefit is increase in search engine traffic and their indirect benefit is having a common framework checklist to use before publishing content on the site.”

SEO can put you ahead of the competition. If two web sites are selling the same thing, the search engine optimized web site is more likely to have more customers and make more sales.
